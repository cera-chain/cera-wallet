## How to reach us

- **Integration / documentation / SLIP-0044**: email above with a clear subject (e.g. `CERA wallet integration`, `SLIP-0044 coin type`).
- **Security vulnerabilities**: see [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) — do not open public issues for sensitive reports.

## Response expectations

We read maintainer mail on a **best-effort** basis. For urgent production incidents affecting your own deployment, include your environment (node version, RPC endpoint, repro steps) in the first message.

## Adding maintainers

When additional maintainers join, update this file with name or team role, contact method, and area of responsibility. Keep at least one reachable email for external integrators and registry requests (e.g. SLIP-0044).
